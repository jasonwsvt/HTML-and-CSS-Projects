I suppose when it gets down to it, it's kinda tough to call myself anything in particular. I spend most of my time right now working on a software developer bootcamp through The Tech Academy. In my free time I really enjoy off-leash trail running with my dog, learning Spanish, listening to audiobooks mostly in the sci-fi/fantasy genre, cooking up gluten free whole food plant based dishes, and getting to a weekly board game night. I hope to experience life in many places around the United States, and perhaps at some point, the world.

I've been coding since I was super young - like five or so years old, when my dad brought home an IBM PC jr. and I spent many hours with BASIC. Years later I would go to the University of Vermont and graduate with a degree in Applied Mathematics and minor in Computer Science. I'm excited to now focus my career path on software development, and the Tech Academy bootcamp should provide a very effective kick-start!

Feel free to check out the site. I have a portfolio of projects, links to my profiles on LinkedIn, Indeed, and GitHub, and a contact form.
